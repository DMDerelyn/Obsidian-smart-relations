# Paladin Class Reference

The Paladin is a holy warrior bound by a sacred oath, combining martial prowess with divine magic. They are among the most versatile classes in 5e, capable of tanking, dealing burst damage, healing, and supporting allies.

## Core Features

### Divine Smite

The Paladin's signature ability. When you hit with a melee weapon attack, you can expend a spell slot to deal **extra radiant damage**:

- 1st-level slot: 2d8 radiant damage
- 2nd-level slot: 3d8 radiant damage
- Each higher slot: +1d8 (max 5d8)
- **Bonus**: +1d8 against undead and fiends

Divine Smite is declared *after* a hit is confirmed, making it resource-efficient. On a critical hit, all smite dice are doubled, creating legendary burst damage potential.

### Lay on Hands

A pool of healing equal to **Paladin level x 5** hit points. You can distribute any amount from this pool with a single action. It can also cure diseases and neutralize poisons (5 HP per effect).

### Aura of Protection (Level 6)

All friendly creatures within **10 feet** (30 feet at level 18) add the Paladin's **Charisma modifier** to all saving throws. This is widely considered one of the strongest features in the game, providing passive party-wide protection.

### Aura of Courage (Level 10)

You and friendly creatures within your aura range **cannot be frightened**. Simple but powerful against dragons, liches, and other fear-inducing enemies.

## Oath Subclasses

Each oath defines the Paladin's moral code and grants unique spells, Channel Divinity options, and high-level abilities:

### Oath of Devotion
The classic holy knight. Grants *Sacred Weapon* (add CHA to attacks) and *Turn the Unholy*. Capstone creates a radiant aura of sunlight.

### Oath of Vengeance
Focused on hunting down evil. Grants *Vow of Enmity* (advantage against one target) and *Relentless Avenger*. Excellent single-target damage dealer.

### Oath of the Ancients
Nature-themed protector. Grants *Aura of Warding* (resistance to spell damage) and fey-themed spells. Outstanding magical durability.

### Oath of Conquest
Fear-based dominator. Grants *Conquering Presence* (frighten enemies) and *Aura of Conquest* (frightened creatures can't move). Strong battlefield control.

### Oathbreaker (DMG)
A Paladin who has broken their oath for dark purposes. Gains #necrotic-power, including an aura that boosts undead allies. Typically an NPC or villain option.

## Multiclassing

Paladins are popular multiclass candidates:

- **Paladin/Warlock**: Short rest spell slots fuel more smites; *Eldritch Blast* adds ranged capability
- **Paladin/Sorcerer**: Sorcery points and metamagic enhance smiting and spell versatility
- **Paladin/Bard**: Expanded spell list and skill proficiencies for a charismatic build

A 2-level dip into Paladin gives any martial character access to Divine Smite and fighting style, making it one of the most impactful short multiclass investments.
