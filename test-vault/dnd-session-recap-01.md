# Session Recap: Into the Shadowfell

**Date Played**: 2026-04-05  
**Party Level**: 9  
**Location**: The Border Ethereal, then the Shadowfell

## Summary

The party followed the trail of the missing scholar Aldric Voss through a weakening planar boundary near the ruins of Thornwatch Keep. Upon crossing into the Shadowfell, the environment shifted dramatically: colors drained to grey, sound became muffled, and a persistent sense of dread settled over the group.

## Key Events

### The Crossing

Elyra the wizard identified the planar rift using *Detect Magic* and determined it had been deliberately torn open. The party debated whether to proceed, but Kael the paladin insisted they could not abandon the missing scholar. The crossing required a DC 15 Constitution saving throw; Brim the halfling rogue failed and gained one level of exhaustion.

### Exploring the Shadow Ruins

Inside the Shadowfell, Thornwatch Keep existed as a **twisted reflection** of its Material Plane counterpart. The walls wept a dark ichor, and shadows moved independently of their sources. The party encountered:

- **3 Shadow Mastiffs** guarding the entrance (dispatched quickly)
- A trapped corridor with necrotic damage glyphs (Elyra disarmed with *Dispel Magic*)
- The scholar's journal, revealing he sought the **Tome of Shadow Binding**

### The Shadow Dragon Encounter

In the keep's great hall, the party confronted a **young shadow dragon** --- a creature warped by centuries of exposure to the Shadowfell's negative energy. Key combat moments:

1. **Initiative**: Kael rolled a natural 20 on initiative, charging in first with Divine Smite
2. **Breath Weapon**: The dragon unleashed its necrotic breath weapon in round 2, dealing 42 damage to Brim and Elyra (both failed DC 17 saves)
3. **Shadow Stealth**: The dragon used *Living Shadow* to hide in dim light, gaining advantage on attacks
4. **Turning Point**: Elyra cast *Daylight* to eliminate the dragon's shadow cover, and Kael followed with two critical smites

The dragon was defeated after 6 rounds of intense combat. Brim nearly died (2 failed death saves) before the cleric Maren stabilized him.

## Loot

- 2,400 gold pieces from the dragon's small hoard
- A *Cloak of Shadows* (requires attunement)
- Fragment of the Tome of Shadow Binding (plot item)

## Notes for Next Session

The fragment suggests the full Tome is hidden in the **Fortress of Memories**, deeper in the Shadowfell. The party needs to decide whether to press on or return to the Material Plane to rest and resupply. Kael's oath compels him to destroy the Tome rather than use it.
